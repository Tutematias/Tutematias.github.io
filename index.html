Hola! Soy Antonella.

My name is Antonella Serrano Costa and I am here to help you speak and understand better the Argentine Spanish variant known as «español argentino». My experience teaching over 2000 Spanish lessons to travelers visiting or moving to Argentina has shown me all the struggles Spanish students face when they come to my country.
